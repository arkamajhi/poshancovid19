Introduction

The POSHAN COVID-19 Resources is an online repository of government circulars, national and international guidelines and technical documents on programming during the COVID-19 Pandemic. This is an easy-to-use compilation of COVID-19 resources focusing on nutrition, food security, early childhood development and related issues. The central and state-level policies since the start of the outbreak and lock-down are also provided in the efforts to restore essential services across the life cycle. The Resources site is updated regularly, providing comprehensive evidence-informed guidance in nutrition and is publicly available for policymakers, researchers, health professionals and general public.

Supported By




Indian Institute of Technology Bombay
PoshanCOVID19 is an initiative of the Centre of Excellence at the Centre for Technology Alternatives for Rural Areas (CTARA), IIT Bombay supported by UNICEF India, IFPRI, WFP and UN India. The website provides at one place all source authenticated information on child nutrition, maternal health and related topics in the context of COVID-19.
